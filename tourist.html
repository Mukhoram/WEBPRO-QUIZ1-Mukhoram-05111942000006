Pekanbaru cannot be separated from Melayunese culture,
including its tourist destinations.

Sang Nila Utama Museum


Sang Nila Utama Museum is a regional museum located in Pekanbaru, Riau. This museum collects and stores heritages related to Riau Malay culture such as traditional wedding clothes, traditional games, musical instruments, artifacts and others.

The Siak Sultan's Shelter House


This historical site is The Siak Sultan's Shelter house of Mr. Kadhi, the Kingdom of Siak, H. Zakaria, which serves as a stopover for the Sultan of Siak Sri Indrapura when he visits Senapelan.
