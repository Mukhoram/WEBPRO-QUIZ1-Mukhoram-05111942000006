Kota Pekanbaru

Pekanbaru City is the capital and largest city in Riau Province, Indonesia. This city is one of the largest economic centers on the island of Sumatra and is a city with a high rate of growth, migration and urbanization. Pekanbaru is located on the banks of the Siak River and was originally a small town with a market (weekend) called Payung Sekaki or Senapelan. Pekanbaru's economy is supported by trade and petroleum mining. The city has an international airport, intercity and interprovincial bus terminals, and two ports.
